Hi there !

I am a computational biologist working on epigenomics and transcriptomics data and now late phase immuno-oncology clinical trial analysis. I did my PhD at the Genome Institute of Singapore as an A * STAR scholar with Dr. Patrick Tan and then a brief postdoc with Mathieu Lupien at University Health Network. I now work as a statistical programmer at Roche Canada.

I most heavily use R and bash scripting for data wrangling, data analysis and visualisation. Sometimes I use Python but I am trying to increase its usage. In previous roles, I have worked with SAS but as you can tell I really do enjoy working in R. With new challenges at work, I have recently picked up google scripting and HTML as well.

I like learning new things and working on problems that aid that end.
